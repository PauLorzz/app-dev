## Alice in Borderland  *(thriller series)* 
>a Japanese Netflix live-action survival-thriller series (not a movie) based on the manga by Haro Aso. The story follows Arisu, a listless gamer trapped in a deserted Tokyo, where he and his allies must compete in fatal, card-themed games to survive and extend their "visas"
### Storyline
Arisu is a man without much money or luck. He is unemployed currently as well. Out of the blue a blinding light engulfs him one day in whose aftermath the city of Tokyo has lost all its inhabitants save Arisu and two friends. This is a dangerous and potentially fatal game of survival now in which they are forced to take part.
- You can watch the series here : [Alice in Borderland](https://www.netflix.com/title/80200575)
